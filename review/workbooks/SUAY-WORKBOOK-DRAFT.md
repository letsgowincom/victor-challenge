## Exercise 4B: The SEAL Framework

*For when something goes wrong. Own it — every time.*

**S — Summarize** what happened (facts, no spin)
**E — Explain** the result (what it cost — time, trust, money, relationship)
**A — Apologize** without shifting blame ("I" statements only)
**L — Let them know** how you'll make it right (specific and time-bound)

**Practice: Think of a recent situation where you could have applied SEAL. Write it out:**

## TEAM ROLLOUT GUIDE

*This section is for leaders who want to bring Show Up As You to their whole team — not just use it for themselves.*

### How to Run This as a Team Experience

**Format:** 7 bi-weekly sessions (1 hour each) — one module per session

**Session structure:**
1. JM's video (15 min)
2. Individual workbook time (15 min)
3. Pair discussion (15 min)
4. Group debrief — what are you taking into the next two weeks? (15 min)

**Between sessions:** Each person completes their workbook exercises and reports back on one thing they did differently.

### Team Agreements (establish these in Session 1)

Before you begin, have the team agree to:

1. What's said in the room stays in the room
2. We give each other the benefit of the doubt
3. We challenge each other — not to be harsh, but because we care about each other's growth
4. Feedback is a gift

**Have everyone sign:**

*"I commit to showing up — for myself and for this team — for the full 7 sessions."*
