*[JM directly to camera — about to challenge a sacred belief]*

"I'm going to say something that might make you uncomfortable.

Work-life balance doesn't exist.

Not for people like you. Not for people who are building something real. The idea that your work and your life are two separate buckets that need to be filled equally — it's a myth. And chasing it is making you miserable.

Here's what I've seen in 16 years of coaching high performers: the people who win — who really win — aren't the ones who found balance. They're the ones who figured out integration.

Integration means your work fuels your life. And your life fuels your work. They're not in competition. They're in conversation.

Today I'm going to show you exactly what that looks like in practice — and why the relationships around you are the key to making it work.

This might be the most personal conversation I have with leaders in my coaching practice. I'm glad you're here for it.

Let's get into it."
