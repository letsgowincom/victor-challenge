*[JM directly to camera, honest and grounded — not selling, confessing]*

"Let me ask you something.

Have you ever sat in a meeting — a room full of successful people — and had this quiet voice in your head say... 'I don't belong here'?

You've built something real. You've earned your seat. And yet — the doubt is there. Whispering that you're going to be found out. That you've just been lucky. That the next project might be the one where everyone sees you're not as good as they think you are.

That's impostor syndrome. And it doesn't just hit new leaders.

In my experience, it hits the best ones hardest. Because the people who care most about doing great work are the same people who are most aware of everything they could still improve.

Today I want to name it, unpack it — and give you the tool to permanently stop letting it run your decisions.

Because I've worked with enough champions to know this: the only difference between someone stuck in impostor syndrome and someone who's broken through it is one simple shift.

Let's get into it."
